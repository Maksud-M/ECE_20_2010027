# Basic Naming Conventions
There are commonly used 4 types of naming conventions:
- Pascal Case.
- Camel Case.
- Snake Case.
- Kebab Case.



## Pascal Case:
ex: `FirstName, LastName`

## Camel Case:
ex: `firstName, lastName`

## Snake Case:
ex: `first_name, last_name`

## Kebab Case:
ex: `first-name, last-name`
